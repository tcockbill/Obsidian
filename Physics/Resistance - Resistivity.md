# Resistance / Resistivity  
0.144 A  
$\text{resistance} \propto \text{length}$  
$\text{resistance} \propto \text{area}$  
$R \propto \frac{L}{A}$  
$R=K \frac{L}{A}$  
$R=\rho \frac{L}{A}$  
  
Intrinsic → always the same independent of the amount  
Extrinsic → depends on the amount  
  
  
$\rho = \frac{\ohm m^2}{m} = \ohm m$
